Our Journey

At Talent Folder, we go beyond recruitment — we build careers, companies, and connections that last. Founded in 2013, Talent Folder has grown into a trusted global HR and workforce solutions partner operating across India, the UAE, Singapore, the UK, and Oman. With over 83+ clients worldwide, we proudly serve diverse industries including Information Technology, Banking, Finance, Operations, Logistics, HR, and Legal.

Recognized as one of the leading HR consulting firms in the UAE and Singapore, Talent Folder delivers a full spectrum of HR services — from recruitment and staffing to workforce management and strategic consulting. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to connect exceptional talent with exceptional opportunities, empowering both people and organizations to grow together.
